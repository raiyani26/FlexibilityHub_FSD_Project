Introduction

This yoga challenge includes 35-minute dynamic classes each day, created to give your whole body a workout. The classes will build in intensity as the challenge progresses. You can expect lots of variety, from 'traditional' Vinyasa flows, to classes that bring in influences from dance, functional yoga, strength training and natural movement.

How to take part:

1. Become an FlexibilityHub Yoga member (if you're not already)

2. Click on the Follow program button above

Join us for the 3 Week Yoga Workout and:

  • Work your body from top to toe
  • Boost your endorphins
  • Improve your strength, fitness and flexibility
  • Increase your energy levels
  • Start or maintain your regular yoga habit
  • Try out new teachers and approaches to movement.
